# Gladstone

Artist, designer, and human development specialist. I spend my days thinking about how people grow — and my evenings zoomed in way too close on 30-year-old pixel art.

I tinker with retro handhelds. Recently that got out of hand, and I ended up building my first piece of homebrew software: **[MiniPlusZoom](https://github.com/hello-gladstone/miniplus-zoom)** — a freeze-and-zoom inspection tool for the Miyoo Mini Plus. It pauses your game and hands you a magnifying glass, because sprite artists from 1994 deserve a closer look.

I came to code the long way around: no computer science background, just curiosity, stubbornness, and a refusal to accept "that's not possible on this device." It turns out that gets you surprisingly far.

**Things I care about:**
- Retro games, and the tiny art hiding inside them
- Design that respects the person using it
- Making technology feel less like a locked door and more like a workbench

**Currently:** enjoying the fact that a zoom tool I built now lives on a device in my pocket.

*If you're into Miyoo / Onion OS homebrew, have a look around — and feel free to open an issue or just say hello.*
